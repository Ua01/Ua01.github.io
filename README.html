About


I'm Ua_01 the creator of this website. I created wild Things with the simple aim of giving life to the hidden world of the natural by educating people about Wild Things. By Letting people know how many different things are out there that we are ignorant of or simply don’t have the time to discover the wonders of the wild. Wild things Lets people explore the wild to learn about plants and insects and how they work and why they are important. A place to test your knowledge on what you know about nature. A place to entertain your self with the many fun games it has to offer.
